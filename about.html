Project Overview

Built with LocalStorage, accessible UI, and delightful themes: Light (bubbles), Dark (honeycomb), Multicolor (aurora).

This app helps you manage tasks, stay focused with Pomodoro, and take care of your health with audio-friendly and notification-aware reminders. Everything is stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage.
